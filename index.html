FAQ

Common Questions

What are your operating hours?+
Premium and VIP members enjoy 24/7 access. Starter members have access from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM, seven days a week, including all public holidays.
Is there a trial period available?+
Yes, we offer a complimentary 3-day trial pass for prospective members. Visit our front desk to activate your trial and experience the facility firsthand.
Can I freeze my membership?+
Members may freeze their membership for up to 30 days per calendar year. Freeze requests require a 7-day notice and can be arranged at the front desk.
Do you offer personal training sessions?+
Absolutely. Our certified personal trainers are available for one-on-one sessions. Premium members receive four sessions monthly, VIP members receive unlimited sessions.
What amenities are included?+
All members have access to locker rooms, showers, and changing facilities. Premium and VIP tiers add steam room, sauna, cold plunge, towel service, and VIP lounge access.
